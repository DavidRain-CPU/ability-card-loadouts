Deadeye

A Moment to Recuperate - Regenerate health at an accelerated rate. Taking any damage will cancel the ability.

Focus Fire - While deadeye is active you and allies deal more damage. Effects do not stack if used by multiple posse members.

Paint it Black - Using deadeye will mark your target(s), and pulling the trigger will shoot all established marks.

Slow and Steady - Using deadeye will make you invulnerable to instant headshot kills. Damage taken is heavily mitigated, but you are restricted to walking.

Quite An Inspiration - While deadeye is active you and allies regenerate health at an accelerated rate. Effects do not stack if used by more than one member.

Slippery Bastard - Using deadeye will not allow enemies to lock onto you, nor for you to lock onto them. Drains your deadeye at a dramatic rate.

Combat

Gunslinger’s Choice - Dual wielding gives you increased damage and accuracy.

Horseman - You deal more damage on horseback.

Sharpshooter - While scoping you do more damage and take less damage.

Necessity Breeds - As your health bar goes down, your damage output goes up.

Landon’s Patience - Waiting up to 15 seconds between shots will increase your damage.

The Short Game - More damage is dealt to closer enemies, and less damage to those further.

Hangman - Your lasso deals damage for every second an enemy is lassoed.

Winning Streak - Each successive shot on the same enemy deals more damage.

Recovery

Iron Lung - Regenerate stamina quicker, and take less damage based on your stamina level. More stamina, less damage taken.

Kick in the Butt - Damage taken is added to your deadeye.

Live for the Fight - Slowly regenerate deadeye over time.

Ride Like the Wind - While mounted, a portion of damage taken or dealt is added to your horse's health and stamina.

Come Back Stronger - Your health begins regenerating sooner after taking damage.

Peak Condition - You deal more damage based on your stamina level (Above 75%).

Eye for an Eye - Headshots restore deadeye.

The Gift of Focus - Items snd abilities that affect deadeye have their effects improved.

Strange Medicine - Regain health when dealing damage. Otherwise health regenerates at half the normal rate.

Cold Blooded - For 5 seconds after killing an enemy, you will regenerate health.

Defense

Fool Me Once - Each consecutive shot you take deals less damage than the previous shot, ends if not shot for 10 seconds.

Friends for Life - While mounted, both you and your horse take less damage.

Strength in Numbers - Take less damage for every nearby ally, stacks to a max of 3.

Hunker Down - Take less damage while taking cover.

To Fight Another Day - Less damage taken from bullets while sprinting.

The Unblinking Eye - Deadeye and Eagle Eye drain slower.

Take the Pain Away - After reviving another player, you both take less damage for 8 seconds.

Of Single Purpose - Take less damage while unarmed or using a melee weapon.

Never without One - Your hat will block a headshot and then fall off, while not wearing a hat you take more damage.

This was created as a catalogue with many practical combinations for different playing styles and ranges, for the benefit of myself and the Red Dead community as a whole.
